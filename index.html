Shariah Compliance: Automated & Auditable

1. Industry Exclusion

  • Conventional Banking
  • Non-Takaful Insurance
  • Alcohol & Tobacco
  • Gambling & Adult Ent.
  • Pork & Weapons

2. Quantitative Filters

  • Debt / Assets: < 33-37%
  • Interest / Revenue: < 5%
  • Illiquid Assets / Assets: > 20%

3. Purification

Any unavoidable impure income is calculated pro-rata and moved to a "charity pot" for user donation.

Screening is performed quarterly on financials and the tradable universe is updated daily. All rules are versioned and auditable by our Shariah Board.
